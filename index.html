About Hope Horizon

Hope Horizon is a compassionate mental health charity dedicated to fostering a world where everyone feels supported and empowered to face life’s challenges. Our mission is to break the stigma surrounding mental health, provide accessible resources, and create a safe space for individuals to heal and thrive.


Our programs are designed to instill hope, resilience, and positivity in the lives of those we serve. Through storytelling initiatives like “Hero Stories,” we celebrate personal triumphs over adversity, inspiring others to seek support and embrace their journeys. Additionally, our "Positive Affirmations" and "Resource Links" sections provide daily motivation and practical tools to nurture mental well-being.


With Hope Horizon, no one has to face their struggles alone. Together, we can build a brighter, more inclusive future where mental health is prioritized and every individual is given the opportunity to flourish.

Information about Mental Health

Mental health issues encompass a wide range of conditions that affect a person’s emotional, psychological, and social well-being. Understanding these issues is the first step toward breaking the stigma and seeking help.

Common Mental Health Issues

  • Anxiety Disorders: Excessive fear or worry that disrupts daily life.
  • Depression: Persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and lack of energy.
  • Bipolar Disorder: Extreme mood swings, including emotional highs (mania) and lows (depression).
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD): Anxiety following traumatic experiences.
  • Eating Disorders: Conditions like anorexia or bulimia, characterized by an unhealthy focus on weight and eating habits.

Strategies for Supporting Mental Health

There are many effective strategies to support mental health and promote well-being:

  • Talk About It: Open conversations reduce stigma and provide emotional relief.
  • Professional Help: Therapy or counseling from trained professionals can make a significant difference.
  • Healthy Lifestyle: Regular exercise, balanced nutrition, and adequate sleep.
  • Mindfulness Practices: Meditation, yoga, and relaxation techniques to manage stress.
  • Community Support: Peer groups and support networks provide understanding and companionship.

Hero Stories

Jonny Benjamin's Story

Jonny Benjamin is a British mental health campaigner who, in 2008, was saved from a suicide attempt by a stranger. He later launched the "Find Mike" campaign to locate and thank the person who saved him, which gained international attention.

Jazz Thornton's Journey

Jazz Thornton, a mental health advocate from New Zealand, survived multiple suicide attempts and now works to raise awareness and support for mental health issues. She co-founded the organization Voices of Hope and has shared her story through various platforms.
